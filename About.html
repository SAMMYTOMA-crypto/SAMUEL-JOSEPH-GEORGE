ABOUT:

Html is a common filename for a webpage that provides information about a website, organization, or individual. Here's a brief overview:
Purpose: The about.html page typically serves as an introduction to:
1. Website owners/ founders
2. Company history
3. Mission and values
4. Team members
5. Contact information


Content: An about.html page may include:

1. Brief bio(s) of key team members
2. Company history and milestones
3. Mission statement and core values
4. Contact details (email, phone, address)
5. Social media links
6. Photos or videos


Importance: The about.html page helps:

1. Establish credibility and trust
2. Provide context for the website's purpose
3. Humanize the organization
4. Facilitate communication and connection


**Best practices:"

1. Keep content concise and engaging
2. Use clear, simple language
3. Include relevant visuals
4. Update information regularly
5. Ensure mobile-friendliness
